Who Is RunicFreak?

RunicFreak is my online handle, specifically for gaming, and entertainment purposes. I have been streaming on twitch for 4 years now, mainly just having fun playing games. Some people don’t understand the purpose of streaming, and to what end it has a use. However, I use it as an escape from boredom. Because I don’t have a lot of people to play games with online, having a live audience while I'm playing make it more fun. Having an audience while its live creates a more competitive gaming experience as well, due to instant feedback from your actions.


Life goes on, except for when it doesn't. But at that point it isnt your problem, so just live god dammit

Although streaming has been a fruitless effort, I had the ability to raise $1000 for the Sheldon Kennedy Advocacy Center through the twitch platform, as well as grow a (small) following that regularly watch. Due to the constant talking during a stream it has also helped with my public speaking skills and increase my sense of creativity. Due to creating thumbnails and looking into the often overlooked to find a joke. Because of these small facts, I don’t think I'll be stopping my streams anytime soon. And I hope I never want to in the future. Stay freaky.
